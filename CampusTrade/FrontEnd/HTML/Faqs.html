Frequently Asked Questions
You ask Queries , We Resolve them!!
How is CampusTrade different from other buy/sell platforms like OLX or Snapdeal?
Unlike traditional buy/sell platforms, CampusTrade is designed specifically for students. We focus on building a community where students can barter items, exchange services, and use CampusCoins to trade without money. Our coin-based system, offline exchange options, and direct student-to-student bartering make CampusTrade more affordable, convenient, and tailored to student life.
Can I sell or trade anything on CampusTrade?
CampusTrade is focused on educational items and services. You can barter or sell textbooks, notes, study materials, project assistance, tutoring, and more. However, it is not designed for general goods outside of the academic context.
What are CampusCoins, and how do they work?
CampusCoins are a form of currency used on CampusTrade. You earn them by completing transactions on the platform, whether through bartering or selling. These coins can be saved and used to buy items or services without using real money.
How can I communicate with other users?
CampusTrade allows you to chat with other users directly online to negotiate and finalize trades. Once the details are set, you can meet offline to exchange items in person.
Can I use CampusTrade if I’m in a different department or division?
Yes, CampusTrade is open to all students, regardless of their department. However, our platform also allows students within the same division to easily arrange barters or offline exchanges, making the process faster and more convenient.
Do I have to pay any fees to use CampusTrade?
No, CampusTrade is completely free to use. There are no fees for registering or completing transactions. Our goal is to provide a student-friendly platform that encourages resource-sharing without the burden of additional costs.
How do I know if the person I’m trading with is trustworthy?
CampusTrade includes user ratings and reviews, so you can check feedback from other users before completing a trade. We recommend always communicating clearly and using our built-in chat feature to avoid any misunderstandings.
